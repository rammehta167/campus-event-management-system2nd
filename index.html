ABOUT URJOTSAV

The Epicenter of Innovation and Culture

Urjotsav is the flagship annual Tech + Cultural + Innovation festival of Prestige Institute of Engineering Management and Research (PIEMR), Indore. Spanning over 8 years of legacy, it is organized every year by PIEMR students and departments to showcase their technical skills, promote innovation, and build a platform for inter-college competition.

This year, we are taking it to the next level with a complete Cyberpunk revamp, heavier prize pools, and state-of-the-art tech arenas across Coding, Robotics, Hackathons, and Entrepreneurship.

"Where creativity meets technology, and sportsmanship meets passion."
